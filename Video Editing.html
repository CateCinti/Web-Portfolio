ENGFLM Video Annotation: The Met Virtual Tour

This video annotation was created for my Visual Literacy course. The purpose of this annotation was to choose a virtual tour from a museum and analyze how the interface of the tour sculpts the viewer's experience. I took relevant clips from The Met's virtual tour on YouTube and added subtitles to annotate each scene. I used ClipChamp to put everything together.
